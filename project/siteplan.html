PriceShare

Website Development Plan

Website Name

"PriceShare" is a clear, memorable name that reflects the platform’s purpose of sharing product prices between companies and customers.

Website Purpose

PriceShare is designed to bridge the gap between retailers and consumers by providing a centralized platform where businesses can share their product pricing information with potential customers. Companies will have authenticated access to upload and manage their product catalogs and pricing, while customers can easily search, filter, and compare products across multiple retailers to find the best purchasing options available to them.

User Scenarios

For Retailers:

  1. How can I efficiently upload and update my product catalog with current pricing information?
  2. What reporting tools will be available to track customer interest in my products?

For Consumers:

  1. How can I quickly find which local stores carry a specific product I need?
  2. What filtering options will be available to compare prices across different retailers?

Color Usage:

  • Serene Blue (Primary Cool): Used for headers, primary buttons, and main navigation. Creates a trustworthy and professional feel.
  • Fresh Green (Secondary Cool): Used for success messages, positive actions, and secondary buttons. Represents growth and freshness.
  • Vibrant Red (Accent Warm): Used for important alerts, sale indicators, and call-to-action elements. Draws attention to key areas.

Typography

Primary Font (Rubik): Used for all headings and important display text. Its geometric yet friendly appearance works well for a price comparison site.

Secondary Font (Inter): Used for body text and general interface elements. Its excellent readability makes it perfect for product descriptions and detailed information.
